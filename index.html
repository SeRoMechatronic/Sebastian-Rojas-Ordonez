A systematic review of 121 studies establishing a unified framework for integrating Cognitive Digital Twins in autonomous robotic systems. Published
Machine Learning and Knowledge Extraction (MDPI MAKE) · Feb 2026

A fine-tuning-free edge framework leveraging Large Language Models deployed on resource-constrained embedded hardware (NVIDIA Jetson) to enhance autonomous robot navigation through zero-shot prompt engineering. Natural-Language Relay Control for a SISO Thermal Plant
Published
Applied Sciences (MDPI) · Dec 2025

A proof-of-concept architecture coupling a Large Language Model to an industrial relay controller for a single-input single-output thermal plant. Validated against a conventional hysteresis controller, the approach demonstrates LLM-based intelligent relay control as a viable paradigm in cyber-physical systems, with natural-language setpoint specification and explainable control decisions.

Research
